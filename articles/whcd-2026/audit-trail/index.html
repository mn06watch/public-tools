EC-07 reclassification — April 27, 2026: A user-surfaced classifier inconsistency around the criminal-identity-as-class-attribute pattern was identified during state-delegation review after the initial run. EC-07 (a Cat 2 clarification covering criminal-identity nouns applied as identity attributes to ethnic, religious, immigration-status, political-affiliation, or institutional classes) was added to the methodology, and 1,239 candidate posts were reclassified by Haiku against the updated prompt. 221 posts flipped from OUT to IN (197 R / 24 D / 0 I); 12 of those flips appear in this audit trail (marked EC-07 in the row detail).
