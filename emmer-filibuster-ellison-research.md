### Characterization Consistency Assessment

Emmer's characterization of the Ellison/fraud story has been **remarkably consistent but with clear escalation** over time:

**Core claims (repeated across all appearances):**
- Walz and Ellison "actively stopped people from investigating" / "retaliated against whistleblowers"
- Feeding Our Future = $250M, largest pandemic fraud case in the country
- Money went to Somalia, potentially to Al-Shabaab

**Escalation pattern:**
- **Early Dec 2025:** "Feeding Our Future" as the primary scandal ($250M)
- **Mid Dec 2025:** Expands to "over $1 billion in fraud" by lumping in Medicaid and housing fraud
- **Late Dec 2025–Jan 2026:** Calls Walz and Ellison "crooks" engaged in "Minnesota thuggery," labels Omar as being "at the center" of the fraud operation
- **Jan 2026:** Compares Walz to Jefferson Davis, calls Omar "hateful, racist, antisemite"

The language has gotten more extreme over time, but the core factual claims have remained the same. What's changed is the rhetorical intensity and the breadth of people pulled into the narrative (from Walz/Ellison → adding Omar → adding Frey).
