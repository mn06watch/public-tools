METHODOLOGY NOTE
================================================================================

This analysis extracted EXACT QUOTES from official transcripts (all from MN-06
Watch, an independent accountability journalism organization that transcribed
these interviews).

WHAT WAS MEASURED:
✓ Language intensity
✓ Personal attacks vs. policy critique
✓ Inflammatory adjectives
✓ Selective emphasis of facts
✓ Scope of discussion
✓ Host pushback responses
✓ Profiling concerns acknowledgment
✓ State vs. federal political focus

WHAT WAS NOT MEASURED:
✗ Factual accuracy (some claims disputed, not fact-checked here)
✗ Audience size/impact
✗ Full-transcript analysis (analyzed what was transcribed)
✗ Non-verbal communication
✗ Interview order/sequence effects

LIMITATION: This analysis shows PATTERNS, not causation. Audience difference could
reflect: interviewer focus, audience expectations, Emmer's deliberate choices, or
combination of factors.
