## METHODOLOGY

- **Tweet search:** 14,605 tweets searched with word-boundary regex to avoid false positives (e.g., "FairandSimple" matching "iran"). 53 original matches, 15 retweet matches.
- **Transcript search:** 810 transcripts searched in summary and key_quotes fields. 17 Iran-specific matches.
- **Sub-search flags:** Applied programmatically across all results using regex patterns for blame attribution, casualty language, "47 years" framing, nuclear references, and cost language.
- **Silence analysis:** Calculated by comparing Iran tweet dates against all tweet dates in the Feb 28–Apr 4 window. Date assignment uses UTC date from tweet timestamp.
- **Caveat:** Transcript summaries and key_quotes are AI-generated from source PDFs and may not capture every quote or nuance. The underlying PDFs may contain additional relevant content.
